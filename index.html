Jenny Della Santa's work can be found in Palette Poetry, Pretty Owl Poetry, and The Hunger. She was an Academy of American Poets College Poetry Prize winner and nominee for Best of The Net and Best New Poets. She has an MFA from Mills College and lives in Oakland with her husband and young daughter.
